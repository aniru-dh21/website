About Us

We Are Here

GRIET - Coding Club

Coding club is a free and open source programming learning community at the GRIET.... The aim of this club is to promote the development of coding skills among the students. Coding club is in a community where you can get help with your coding project, or just hang out with other people who are learning how to code
If you want to learn how to program, join us!

Vision

To be an epicentre for creating a healthy competitive coding atmosphere among the students.

Mission

To achieve and impart competitive programming skills in students by guiding and encouraging peer learning.

GRIET - Coding Club

To promote the importance of coding and assist students to improve their problem solving skills. Coding is an essential skill that can help you land a job in the future, and it is also an important part of our future economy.
